Research Paper Preparation Guidelines.

Paper Submission: The Articles can be submitted by two methods; first direct email to us at  submission@ajonsr.com  or through the manual submission form. If you are experiencing any kind of technical difficulty in the manual form submission, kindly email us your paper. Both method of submission will be given equal importance. It should be clearly noted that submission does not guarantee acceptance of manuscript for publication as it is still subject to review by our review board.

 

Paper Layout: Authors are advised to prepare their Manuscript in a separate A4 sized document and then cut or copy their article and paste it in our Template. Other option is to use our Guidelines for writing research work from beginning. Research papers are ONLY accepted in ENGLISH language and Font face is TIMES NEW ROMAN only. Major font and paragraph specifications are given in the TABLE below.   Numbering style may be Number or Roman Numerals depending on the author's choice. Heading and Sub-heading of sections may be written as I. INTRODUCTION or 1. Introduction, it may be Uppercase or Sentence case. Try to keep Manuscript precise and NOT MORE THAN 5 to 8 pages. All body text is justified (not heading and sub heading). Please download our Guidelines for writing research work for the full details of our Manuscript writing Procedure. Some details are given in the tables below.

Types of Articles: All submitted articles should be authors' original work, not considered for publication in other Journals, it should be experimental data or theoretical application. Article submitted to journal Should meet the journal's rigorous peer review standard before we recommend it for publication. Authors are advised not to submit Plagiarized materials. Some of the major types of article published by our journal are given below:

 

a) Original Research: These articles should clearly and precisely describe the original research work. It should describe the methodologies used and experiments done before obtaining new findings. It should be accurate and complete in itself so that others can easily verify the work.

 

b) Short Note: Short note usually describes new idea and innovation, it may not be a complete full length paper. Usually short notes show change in scenario due to some technological advancement in a particular area.

 

c) Survey/Review Articles: Review articles show literature survey of particular area/problem in an organised and precise manner while Survey paper gives the common comprehensive outcome from many research work analysis.

 

Copyright Transfer Details: Corresponding authors should fill the copyright form by using ink pen and sign it for transfer of copyright of article to Research Publish Journals. Now scan this copyright form and send this soft copy (image file or pdf format) along with the Paper/Manuscript by email or through form submission. Author can download our Copyright Form.

 

Submission Terms: Authors are advised to agree to and read carefully the terms and conditions, privacy policy, and disclaimers of this website before submitting their manuscript for publication. The corresponding author(s) is/are solely responsible for the approval granted by all co-authors for submitting manuscript. Article contains no such material that may be unlawful, infringe any proprietary or personal rights of others (including and without limitation, any copyrights or privacy rights); that the Work is factually accurate and contains no matter libelous or otherwise unlawful.
